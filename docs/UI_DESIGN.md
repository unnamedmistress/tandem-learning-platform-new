# UI Design Specification

## Visual Directions

### Modern & Minimalist
- **Mood:** Clean, efficient, and professional.
- **Colors:** Cool tones with accent colors like #2F80ED (blue) and #BDBDBD (gray).
- **Typography:** Sans-serif fonts like Arial or Helvetica.
- **Vibe:** Sleek and unobtrusive.

### Friendly & Engaging
- **Mood:** Warm, approachable, and interactive.
- **Colors:** Earthy tones with #F2994A (orange) and #6FCF97 (green) highlights.
- **Typography:** Rounded fonts like Poppins.
- **Vibe:** Inviting and conversational.

### Tech-Savvy & Bold
- **Mood:** Futuristic and daring.
- **Colors:** Dark background with neon accents like #56CCF2 (light blue) and #BB6BD9 (purple).
- **Typography:** Bold font choices like Montserrat.
- **Vibe:** Dynamic and inspiring.

## Chosen Direction
Selected **Friendly & Engaging** for its capability to create an inviting user experience conducive to learning and reflection.

## Component Inventory
- **Buttons:** Primary, secondary, tertiary
- **Cards:** Content cards with images
- **Inputs:** Text fields, drop-downs, sliders
- **Chat Bubbles:** User and AI conversation
- **Tokens:** Achievement badges
- **Modals:** Notifications and confirmations

## Color System
- Primary: #F2994A (orange)
- Secondary: #6FCF97 (green)
- Background: #F2F2F2 (light gray)
- Text: #333333 (dark gray)

## Typography Scale
- **Headings:** H1 36px, H2 30px, H3 24px
- **Body Text:** 16px
- **Captions:** 12px

## Interaction States
- **Hover:** Slight color darkening
- **Active:** Bold underline
- **Disabled:** Grayed out (#E0E0E0)
- **Focus:** Border glow effect

## Bold Creative Choice
**Dynamic Skill Tokens** - Implement animated token rewards when users achieve milestones, ensuring they are visually captivating yet informative.
